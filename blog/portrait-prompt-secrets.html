5 Secrets to Perfect AI Portrait Prompts

Blog · 6 min read

After generating thousands of AI portraits, we've identified five patterns that separate amateur prompts from professional-grade results. These secrets will transform your AI portraits instantly.

Secret 1: Lighting Direction Matters More Than You Think

Most prompts just say "good lighting" — but specifying the direction and quality of light dramatically changes how the face is perceived. "Soft window light from the left at a 45-degree angle" creates a completely different look than "bright studio lighting."

Secret 2: Describe Skin Texture, Not Just Color

Instead of just "fair skin," professionals describe texture: "smooth fair skin with a subtle natural glow, faint freckles across the nose bridge, and warm undertones visible in sunlight." This level of detail prevents the AI from generating generic skin.

💡 Secret 3: Camera Language Works

Adding camera terms like "85mm lens, f/1.8, shallow depth of field, creamy bokeh" tells the AI to render in a photographic style rather than an illustrative one. This single trick upgrades image quality dramatically.

Secret 4: Layer Your Descriptions

Structure your prompt in layers: style → subject → face → hair → clothing → lighting → camera → background. This hierarchical approach helps the AI prioritize important details and produces more coherent results.

Secret 5: Negative Prompts (When Available)

If your AI generator supports negative prompts, use them to exclude common artifacts: "blurry, deformed, extra fingers, asymmetrical eyes, plastic skin, cartoon, illustration." This prevents the AI from falling into common failure modes.
