Best Prompt for Same Face AI

Guide·6 min read

Want the same face to appear in every AI image you generate? The secret lies in your prompt structure. Here's the exact template format that professional AI artists use.

The Winning Prompt Structure

  • Style tag: "Ultra-realistic photography, 8K resolution"
  • Face block: Complete facial description (skin, eyes, nose, lips, face shape)
  • Hair block: Color, length, texture, style, highlights
  • Body block: Skin tone visible on neck/hands, body type
  • Lighting: Direction, quality (soft/hard), color temperature
  • Camera: Depth of field, bokeh, focal length suggestion
  • Scene: Background, environment, mood — this is what you change between images

💡 The Golden Rule

Never change the Face Block, Hair Block, or Body Block between images. Only modify the Scene section. This is the single most important rule for face consistency.

Example Template

"Ultra-realistic portrait photography, 8K, sharp focus. A person with warm olive skin, oval face, defined cheekbones, dark brown almond eyes with winged eyeliner, straight nose, full pink lips. Shoulder-length wavy dark brown hair with caramel highlights, center part. Soft natural window light from the left, shallow depth of field, creamy bokeh. [CHANGE THIS PART: standing in a sunlit cafe, wearing a navy blazer, reading a book...]"

How Our Tool Creates This

Instead of manually writing these detailed prompts, our AI analyzes your photo and generates the complete prompt automatically. Every facial feature is extracted and described in forensic detail — giving you a ready-to-use, identity-preserving prompt in seconds.

Tips for Maximum Consistency

  • Use the same AI generator for all images in a series
  • Keep the seed number consistent if your generator supports it
  • Use the --cref (character reference) flag in Midjourney alongside our prompts
  • Avoid changing the lighting description between prompts
  • Test with 3–5 images first to verify consistency before large batches
