AI Face Consistency Guide

7 min read · Intermediate

Face consistency is the holy grail of AI image generation. Whether you're creating a character for a story, building a brand, or generating professional headshots, keeping the same face across multiple images is essential. Here's the complete guide.

Step 1: Start with a Quality Reference Photo

Your reference photo is the foundation. It should be:

  • Well-lit with even lighting (avoid harsh shadows)
  • Front-facing or slightly angled (avoid profile shots)
  • High resolution (at least 512×512 pixels)
  • Clear and sharp (no blur or motion artifacts)
  • Neutral or natural expression

Step 2: Generate an Identity-Preserving Prompt

Use our tool to analyze your reference photo. The AI will extract every facial detail and create a prompt with a built-in face lock. This is far more effective than writing prompts manually because the AI vision model catches details humans miss.

🎯 Key Insight

The difference between a good prompt and a great one is specificity. "Brown eyes" is vague. "Warm amber-brown irises with a subtle golden ring around the pupil, slightly almond-shaped with a gentle upward tilt at the outer corners" — that's what produces consistency.

Step 3: Use Platform-Specific Tools

Each AI platform has its own consistency features:

  • Midjourney: Use --cref URL with your reference image URL. Set --cw 100 for maximum face consistency.
  • Flux: Use IP-Adapter with your reference image. Set the adapter weight to 0.7–0.9 for best results.
  • Stable Diffusion: Use ControlNet with IP-Adapter or Reference-Only mode. Combine with a detailed prompt.

Step 4: Lock Your Settings

Once you get a result you like, keep these settings identical:

  • Seed number (if the platform supports it)
  • Model version
  • Style settings
  • The core face description in your prompt

Step 5: Iterate and Refine

Generate 4–8 variations and pick the best one. If the face drifts, go back to your reference photo and regenerate the prompt. Small adjustments to the face description can make a big difference.
